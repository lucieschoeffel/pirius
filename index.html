About Pirius

Our Online Language Training Center was founded by former employees of other language schools from all over Germany. We ourselves worked in administration as administrative specialists, working students and managers. At the same time, some of us were teaching courses as language teachers and we were course participants ourselves in order to further our language skills for our professional and private lives. By taking this all-around look, we were able to understand what methods needed to be improved to give you, the participant, the most effective learning experience.
