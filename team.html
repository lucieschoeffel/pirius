About Me

As the founder of Pirius, I have been helping foreign students learn German since I was just 15 years old. I understand the struggle of feeling limited in the way you can express yourself if you´re not fluent in the language of your peers. That's why it's a necessity to have access to language coaches like me, who help you find your own voice to tell your story. I have experience as an editor, ghostwriter, and tutor. Today, more than ten years later, I'm going my own way with Pirius and taking all of you with me.
